**1225705283*Clione breeding
I was quite surprised to read a description of an interior aquarium called <a href="http://click.j-a-net.jp/1050611/259085/" target="_blank">Curio<img src="http://text.j-a-net.jp/1050611/259085/" height ="1" width="1" border="0"></a>, which was quite surprising when I read the description of the interior aquarium.
When I saw the phrases "keeping clione" and "interior aquarium," I thought it was a clione version of the common interior aquarium with fake jellyfish in it, but it was not at all.

>>
The water temperature is always fixed at 3°C when the indoor temperature is 28°C or lower by CPU control. The water supply and drainage integrated filtration system (our patented structure) generates convection currents that allow cliones to spend their time stress-free, reproducing the Sea of Okhotsk under the ice floes where cliones live.
<<

Because! They say you can seriously keep clione at home. The FAQ is quite interesting. The FAQs are quite interesting: clione larvae are bud-shaped, they don't have wings yet, so they move around with cilia, and clione are hermaphrodites. I wonder if we can also observe predation scenes.
