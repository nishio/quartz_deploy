It's already 9/9. I am a little relieved to be done with the deadly schedule of three presentations (three 40-minute, three 800-person, and three 40-minute presentations) in about 10 days because I took on too much unintentionally.

The house is in stunning disrepair. There is so much clutter that there is no place to step. I haven't been able to deal with the leftover vegetables I bought the weekend before to make a potluck. There is a small fly somewhere. The garbage bag was giving off a strange smell, so I threw it out. (Today is burnable garbage day.) When I wasn't cooking for myself, I could leave the garbage bag until it was full and take it out once a month or once every two months, but not when I start handling raw food. I think it's been maybe 3 weeks and it already smelled pretty bad.

Also, both the lights in the bathroom and the toilet went out. I think they have gone out twice in a year and a half, but I don't know if they are that bad. Maybe it's not the right model.
