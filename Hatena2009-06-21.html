Venue Now. It is difficult to know when to give an explanation. If I start explaining at the moment they arrive, people who want to see the work without any preconceived notions will be troubled.


And that's the other thing. Perhaps it is because I am an engineer, but I seem to estimate too highly the value of the pleasure I feel the moment I realize, "Ah, I see. I learned about <a href="http://shuheiyamada.com/">Shuhei Yamada</a>'s work yesterday when we were talking, and it was very interesting for me to hear what he intended when I thought, "I see," but he never wrote an explanation of what he was doing. I don't write explanations of "this is what it is. I guess I shouldn't be so active in explaining.
