Elementary school kids are so cute.
By the way, I've seen a few comments here and there saying that the comments are not mature, but I think they are not. This is an age when a 5th grader can post a video with audio and get this much attention. It is hard to imagine that he is the only child in his class with Internet access, and it is also hard to imagine a 5th grader doing something so flashy and not talking to his classmates about it. If there is only one child who uses Hatena, and that child has only a DSi, would there be a trend of "Put millions of Hatena Stars on it! would happen? If there are several children who have used Hatena Hike or similar services, and getting a lot of Hatena Stars is equivalent to catching a big beetle in the old days, this kind of phenomenon will not occur unless it is recognized as a value among children.
