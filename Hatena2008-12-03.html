*1228305663* pot diary
If you buy the base of the pot, it costs about 250 yen, so I'll make it without buying it to save money.

First, I put a pack I bought a long time ago into 2 liters of water to take out the broth. The expiration date is June 2007. I've left it at room temperature, but it seems to be OK.

After the soup stock has been extracted, the next step is to add motsu (pork). Simmer it. When it's done, I taste it. Wow, this is terrible, there is no taste. I added 100 ml of 3 times diluted soup stock. It tastes light, but it's okay. I add another 100ml of water to the vegetables.
