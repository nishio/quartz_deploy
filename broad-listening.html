broad listening

Sep 07, 20245 min read

The development of technology to duplicate and distribute information has made it easier for one person to communicate ideas to a large number of people. However, when a large number of people transmit information, the volume of information received by one person becomes enormous, and the person drowns in the flood of information. In a society and company where people actively communicate, technology that supports listening to the opinions of many will be important.
