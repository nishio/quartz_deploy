*1192033811* Pachelbel's Canon in Hatsune Miku (with some lyrics)

On my way home this day, I suddenly remembered that I had seen a video of Hatsune Miku making Pachelbel's Canon, and I thought it was a waste of time because she only said "ah", which is almost meaningless. But I got tired of it halfway through, or ran out of energy, or something, and Hatsune got drunk, so I'm back to "A" again. Well, I'll do something when I feel like it.
http://www.nishiohirokazu.org/files/canon.mp3
The reason why the notes seem to be shaking in the second half is because the default setting for portamento, which is a smooth connection between notes, was set. If you want to play canon seriously, you need to turn off portamento and vibrato in the default setting of notes first. Also, the three of them are like three sisters with gender factors of 0, +30, and -30 respectively. But it's not good. First of all, the first note doesn't cover the range of the canon.
